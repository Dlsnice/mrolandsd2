Our Unique Expertise
Development Support & Project Guidance

We help developers obtain approvals and keep documentation, and project requirements organized from early stages through the point at which the association is ready to operate. We coordinate with you and your sales team, title and escrow officers, engineers, attorneys, and more to create and maintain entitlements for your project.

DRE Submittals through Final Public Report

We handle the DRE submittal process for you. Including obtaining Preliminary, Conditional, and Final Public Reports as applicable and tracking the project timelines for required amendments and renewals.

Document & Budget Creation

We have in-house counsel to create your CC&Rs, custom Purchase Agreements, Grant Deeds, Easements, etc. In addition, we have experienced staff to create your proforma HOA budgets and complete the application packages on your behalf.

Turnover Coordination & Administrative Support

We ensure a smooth transition from developer to homeowner control by organizing responsibilities, timelines, and records. We also support governance by preparing and attending board and annual meetings, sending agendas in advance, publishing minutes, and managing all administrative follow-through.
